Playlist Manager

Organize, view and create Spotify playlists easily!

Playlist Manager merges the songs of selected playlists into one view, allowing you to effortlessly add and remove songs from several playlists.

Be updated with the new songs of many different playlists you follow at the same time, by ordering them by recently added among them.

Login to Spotify to try it out!
